# MotorGo Mini

**Kickstarter-launched gimbal motor controller platform**

<!-- status: Shipped · Kickstarter funded -->

The MotorGo Mini combines a gimbal motor controller with a power stage, ESP32 microcontroller, and Qwiic sensor connectivity — all on a single compact board.

## What It Does

A full robot balancing platform out of the box. Plug in a gimbal motor and sensors, flash some code, and you have a self-balancing robot. No breadboards, no wiring nightmares, no "step 1: spend 3 hours on power electronics."

## Why It Matters

This was the first board I designed with the philosophy that **the hardware itself should teach you**. The form factor invites experimentation — connect different sensors via Qwiic, try different control algorithms, break things and learn.

## Key Specs

- ESP32 microcontroller
- Integrated gimbal motor driver + power stage
- Qwiic/I2C sensor connector
- Compact single-board design
